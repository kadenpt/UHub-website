UHub

What is UHub?

UHub is an upcoming student engagement platform, focused on consolidating student information and opportunities such as campus events, job and research opportunities, and more! Students are enabled to select an affiliation and view opportunities at their university. Organizations are encouraged to post events and opportunities such as hirings, seminars, and other gatherings.
