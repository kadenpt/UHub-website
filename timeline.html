UHub

Your University, Your Hub

Timeline

Pre Beta Launch

Present until Late June

As the development of UHub progresses, UHub is seeking beta testers for the Summer semester, as well as feedback and input regarding functionality and other insights that may be valuable to the development team. If you are part of a club or enrolled in summer classes, you are strongly encouraged to sign-up for email updates below.

Beta Launch

Late June to August

UHub will officially begin its beta launch for the Summer semester. Over this period, details and functionality will be finalized based on the beta testers input. If you are interested in being a beta tester, sign-up on the Learn more page!

Full Launch

September

For the Fall semester, UHub will officially launch for public use at the University of Britsh Columbia. Afterwards, other universities will be considered for deployment. If you are interested in becoming a user and receiving updates, as well as providing input on the functionality and design of the app, sign-up on the Learn More page. Furthermore, if you are part of a club or organization interested in using the app, also sign up!

Disclaimer: Current deployment timeline is strictly for UBC Vancouver.
